AUTOMOTIVE GLASS

Do you really want take a gamble with some fly by night company? Deru's Glass Works specializes in auto glass replacements, windshields, windshield repairs, side and back glass replacements, as well as rear and side view mirror repairs. Our trained professionals will replace your windshield using the best method possible. Our installers are certified by Essex. We use only first quality automotive glass manufactured by some of the best in the industry such as LOF, PPG, Carlite, and Guardian to name just a few. We will replace your windshield to original OEM standards. We only use "full cut-out method" and the finest adhesives available. We will never use a "quick set" method on your car. We know how you value your vehicle and we treat each one as if it were our own. So, knowing all of this, why would you gamble with your vehicle and possibly your family's safety by trusting anyone but Deru's Glass Works???
